WHAT IS CLEAR FUNDS?

Clear Funds aims to provide donation transparency by providing a direct path for your donation to services of your choosing within the charity to which you wish to contribute. This keeps your donation dollars out of the general cash pool whose allocation depends solely on the management of the charity. Clear Funds gives you the power to bypass the possibility of charity fraud or suspicious budget allocation by donating to individual services directly.


WHAT IS CHARITY FRAUD?

Charity fraud is the act of using deception to get money from people who believe they are making donations to charities. Often a person or a group of people will make material representations that they are a charity or part of a charity and ask prospective donors for contributions to the non-existent charity.
