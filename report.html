Report Charity Fraud

If you suspect fraudulent activity within any charitable or non-profit organization,
these are helpful resources for reporting any suspicious activities:



"CharityWatch, founded 25 years ago as the American Institute of Philanthropy (AIP),
is America's most independent, assertive charity watchdog. CharityWatch does
not merely repeat what a charity reports using simplistic or automated formulas.
We dive deep to let you know how efficiently a charity will use your donation to
fund the programs you want to support. CharityWatch exposes nonprofit abuses
and advocates for your interests as a donor."


"Founded in 2001, Charity Navigator has become the nation's largest
and most-utilized evaluator of charities. In our quest to help donors,
our team of professional analysts has examined tens of thousands of
non-profit financial documents. We've used this knowledge to develop
an unbiased numbers-based rating system to assess over 8,000 of
America's best-known and some lesser known, but worthy, charities."
